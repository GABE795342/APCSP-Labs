Part II: Understand Vectors: Magnitude and Direction

  • Magnitude and Direction

  • Think of the magnitude of a vector as the length of the hypotenuse of a right triangle.
    If the x and y properties of your vector object represent the sides of the triangle, what is the magnitude of the vector in terms of x and y? 

    this.loc.mag();  // returns the magnitude of vector loc

  • Think of the direction of a vector as the angle formed by the hypotenuse and one side of the triangle
    If the x and y properties of your vector object represent the sides of the triangle, what is the direction of the vector in terms of x and y? 

    this.loc.dir();  // returns the direction of vector loc

Part II: Understand Vectors: Addition and Subtraction

  • To add vectors, simply add the x properties and y properties

  • You can use the add() method in the vector object
